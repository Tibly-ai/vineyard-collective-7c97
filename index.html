Our Story

Three Generations, One Hillside

Vineyard Collective began as a single hillside plot and a promise: to let the land speak before the cellar ever does. Today our estate spans twenty-two acres of dry-farmed vines, tended by hand and harvested by instinct rather than the calendar.

We make small lots of wine the old way — native yeasts, neutral oak, and patience measured in years, not months. The result is wine with a sense of place, poured the way it was meant to be shared: slowly, and among friends.
